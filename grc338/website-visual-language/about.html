Our Story

Our story began with two people who saw beauty everywhere—especially in each other. What started as weekend trips to local flower markets slowly grew into a shared dream. Between early-morning coffee runs, late-night arranging sessions, and countless moments spent marveling at nature’s colors, we realized we wanted to create a space where others could experience the same joy flowers brought into our lives.

We founded Wild Petal Studio as a way to blend our passions: creativity, craft, and connection. Every bouquet, centerpiece, and arrangement is rooted in the belief that flowers are more than decoration—they’re a language of emotion, a small act of care, and a way to celebrate life’s everyday magic. Together, we honed our style: natural, airy, and slightly wild, inspired by the landscapes we grew up exploring.

Today, our studio is a reflection of our partnership—equal parts artistry and heart. We work side-by-side to bring your floral visions to life, whether you’re gifting a single stem or planning a special celebration. Thank you for supporting our little love-built business. We’re honored to share our craft, our story, and our blooms with you.
